Person with Disability Section

City Social Welfare Department, City Hall, Cagayan de Oro City

Qualifications

  • Must be 59 years old below
  • Resident of Cagayan de Oro only
  • Must be a Filipino Citizen
  • Specific Type of Disability if in case of Chronic illness

Requirements

FOR NEW

  • Brgy. Certificate of Residency
  • Medical Certificate Confirming client's Disability
  • 1x1 ID Picture
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate of PWD
  • Specimen Signature (White Background)

FOR RENEWAL

  • Brgy. Certificate of Residency
  • Old PWD ID
  • Medical Certificate Confirming client's Disability
  • 1x1 ID Picture
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate of PWD
  • Specimen Signature (White Background)

FOR LOST ID

  • Brgy. Certificate of Residency
  • Medical Certificate Confirming client's Disability
  • 1x1 ID Picture
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate of PWD
  • Affidavit of Loss
  • Specimen Signature (White Background)
